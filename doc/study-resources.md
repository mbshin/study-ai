## Math (From the Basics)

### Khan Academy (Free, Self-Paced)

- [Pre-Algebra](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/pre-algebra) — Fractions, exponents, logs, graphing
- [Algebra 1 & 2](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra) — Variables, functions, slopes, equations
- [Linear Algebra](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/linear-algebra) — Vectors, matrices, transformations
- [Calculus](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/calculus-1) — Derivatives, chain rule, gradients
- [Statistics & Probability](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability) — Distributions, Bayes, hypothesis testing

### Visual Explanations (YouTube)

- [3Blue1Brown — Essence of Linear Algebra](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) — Best visual intro to vectors and matrices
- [3Blue1Brown — Essence of Calculus](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDMsr9K-rj53DwVRMYO3t5Yr) — Intuition over formulas
- [3Blue1Brown — Neural Networks](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDNU6R1_67000Dx_ZCJB-3pi) — How neural nets actually work, visually
- [StatQuest](https://www.youtube.com/@statquest) — Statistics and ML concepts explained simply

### Interactive

- [Seeing Theory](https://seeing-theory.brown.edu/) — Interactive probability and statistics visualizations
- [Interactive Linear Algebra](https://textbooks.math.gatech.edu/ila/) — Free textbook with interactive demos

## AI and Machine Learning

- [fast.ai — Practical Deep Learning](https://www.fast.ai/) — Top-down, code-first approach
